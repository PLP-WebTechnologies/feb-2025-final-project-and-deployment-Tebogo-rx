What is Responsive Design?

Posted on May 5, 2025 by Alex

Responsive design ensures your website looks good on phones, tablets, and desktops. It's achieved through flexible layouts, media queries, and adaptive elements.

Why It Matters

More than half of web traffic comes from mobile devices. If your site isn't responsive, you're losing visitors.

How to Get Started

  • Use percentage widths instead of fixed pixels.
  • Apply media queries to adjust styles by screen size.
  • Test your site on multiple devices or with browser dev tools.
